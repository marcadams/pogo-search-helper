How Search Strings Work in Pokemon GO

Pokemon GO's storage search uses special syntax:

  • & means AND — both conditions must match (narrows results)
  • , means OR — either condition matches (broadens results)
  • ! means NOT — exclude matches from results

Popular Search String Examples

  • 3*,4*&!shadow&!purified — High IV Pokemon for raids
